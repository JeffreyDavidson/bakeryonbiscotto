# Bakery on Biscotto — LLC & Business Setup Checklist

## LLC Formation
- [ ] File Florida LLC at sunbiz.org (~$125)
- [ ] Get EIN from IRS (irs.gov, free, instant)
- [ ] Open business bank account (separate from personal)
- [ ] Register "Bakery on Biscotto" as fictitious name/DBA if LLC name differs

## Insurance (Recommended)
- [ ] General liability insurance (covers customer claims)
- [ ] Product liability insurance (covers food-related illness claims)
- [ ] Look into cottage food specific policies (some insurers specialize)

## Compliance
- [ ] Verify Florida cottage food requirements are met
- [ ] Cottage food label requirements (if not already done)
- [ ] Keep revenue tracking under $250K annual cap (already built in KneadIt!)

## Financial
- [ ] Move PayPal Business account under the LLC
- [ ] Track all business expenses through business bank account
- [ ] Keep personal and business finances separated
- [ ] Consider quarterly estimated tax payments as revenue grows

## Legal Pages (Website)
- [ ] Terms & Conditions for orders
- [ ] Allergy disclaimer (already in admin Settings ✅)
- [ ] Privacy Policy (if collecting customer data online)
